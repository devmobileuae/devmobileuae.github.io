Pix App Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 28 May 2025

Thank you for using Pix, a real-time image pixelization and styles app for Android, created and operated by an individual developer. Your privacy is important. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled in the Pix app.

Authentication and Account Information

Pix offers sign-in via Google or email and password, using Firebase Authentication as a secure authentication provider. When you sign in, certain basic information such as your email address, display name, and profile picture (for Google Sign-In) is collected and securely managed by Firebase.

  • Your authentication data is used solely for providing access to app features and subscriptions.
  • Your password (if using email + password) is never visible to the app developer and is securely stored by Firebase.
  • Your account information is not shared with any third parties or used for marketing purposes.
  • For more information, see Firebase Privacy and Security.

Account Deletion

You may delete your Pix account at any time:

  • Via the app’s Profile page, using the “Delete Account” option (if available in your version).
  • Or by sending a deletion request from your registered email address to devmobileuae@gmail.com. Your account and associated authentication information will be permanently deleted from Firebase within 30 days of your request.

After your account is deleted, your authentication information (email, display name, and profile picture) will be removed and cannot be recovered. This action is permanent.

Information Collection

Pix does not collect or store any personal data, images, or user content on external servers. All image processing happens on your device in real time.

  • Images: Your photos are processed locally and never leave your device.
  • Analytics: Pix may use anonymous analytics (such as Google Play Console statistics) to understand general app usage (e.g., installs, crashes). No personal or identifying data is collected.
  • Payments: Subscriptions and payments are managed securely through Google Play. Pix does not process or store your payment details.

Data Sharing

Pix does not share your data with third parties. Your images, edits, and any app usage data remain private.

Permissions

Pix requests permission to access your camera and photos only to allow you to pixelate images and apply styles. These permissions are used solely for app functionality.

Children's Privacy

Pix is not intended for children under 13. No information is knowingly collected from children.

Changes to Policy

This policy may be updated in the future. Significant changes will be announced in the app or on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, contact: alex.pix.app@gmail.com
